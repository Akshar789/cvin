Fix Template Recommendation Confusion – Premium Must Clearly Outperform Free

The recommendation logic is still confusing.

Current Problem:

After selecting a premium template:

Other templates show things like:

“18% Upgrade”

Free template shows 75% match

As a user, this creates confusion.

It feels like the free template might be better than premium.

This destroys the perception of value.

🎯 We Need a Clear & Strong Strategy

We must completely redesign the scoring and messaging logic.

✅ New Clear Rule (Non-Negotiable)

Premium templates must always:

Show match score 90% and above

Be labeled as:

“ATS Optimized”

“High Impact Layout”

“Recruiter Preferred”

“Professional Upgrade”

Free templates must:

Show match between 65% – 75%

Be positioned as:

“Good Match”

“Basic Layout”

“Standard Structure”

Free template must NEVER:

Show higher match than premium

Show “better” than premium

Display upgrade logic against premium

🧠 Change the Logic Approach

Instead of comparing template vs template,
compare template vs career optimization level.

Premium = Optimized Structure
Free = Basic Structure

Scoring model example:

Free:
65% – 75%

Premium:
90% – 96%

No overlap.
No equality.
No contradiction.

🚫 Remove Confusing Messaging

Remove:

“+15% better”

“Upgrade +18%”

Any relative comparison between free and premium.

Instead show:

For premium:
✔ Best Fit for Your Experience
✔ ATS Optimized
✔ Structured for Mid/Senior Level
✔ Higher Recruiter Impact

For free:
✔ Good Starting Template
✔ Basic Clean Layout

🎨 UX Messaging Should Feel Like This

If user selects free template:

Premium suggestion should feel like:
“Upgrade to unlock optimized layout tailored to your experience.”

If user selects premium template:

Do NOT show free template as competitive.
Free should feel like downgrade.

✅ Expected Final Result

✔ Premium templates always appear superior
✔ No confusing percentages
✔ Clear hierarchy between free and premium
✔ Strong upgrade psychology
✔ ATS-focused positioning
✔ No contradictory match values
✔ System feels intelligent and strategic
