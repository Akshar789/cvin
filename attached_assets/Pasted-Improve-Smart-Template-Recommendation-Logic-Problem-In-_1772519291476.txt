Improve Smart Template Recommendation Logic
🎯 Problem

In the Template Recommendation step, I noticed a major issue with the match percentage logic.

Currently:

The user’s selected template sometimes shows 95% match.

Suggested templates show 65%, 75%, 80%, etc.

Sometimes even premium templates show lower match than the selected free template.

This creates a bad impression:

It looks like:

The premium template is worse.

The AI recommendation is weak.

The system is not smart.

There is no strong reason to switch templates.

This defeats the purpose of a recommendation system.

❌ Why This Is a Problem

If the selected template has 95% match and suggestions are lower:

Why would user switch?

Why would user upgrade?

It looks like we are downgrading.

It does not feel AI-powered.

It feels random.

✅ Required Smarter Approach

We need to redesign the recommendation logic so that:

Suggested templates must always:

Look better.

Feel more structured.

Appear more optimized.

Show stronger match than current selection.

Match score strategy must be intelligent:

Current template should not always dominate.

Suggested template (especially premium) should show higher or optimized match.

Match values must feel logical and aspirational.

Suggested templates should:

Be ranked based on:

Experience level

Career stage (Student vs Professional)

Years of experience

Job domain

Content density

Section richness

Highlight structural advantages:

Better hierarchy

Better section distribution

More professional layout

More readable format

🧠 Smarter Recommendation Strategy

Instead of just static % match:

Show improvement-based scoring.

Show “Optimized for your experience level”

Show “Better suited for mid-level professionals”

Show “More impact-driven layout”

Show “ATS-friendly layout”

For premium templates:

Highlight clear advantages.

Make them feel upgraded.

Make match score slightly higher than selected template when logically valid.

Avoid making premium appear weaker.

🎨 UX Improvement

Current template can show:
“Your Current Selection”

Suggested template can show:
“Recommended Upgrade”
“Better Alignment”
“Stronger Impact Layout”

Match % must support that logic.

🚫 Do NOT

Do not randomly inflate numbers.

Do not mislead.

Do not downgrade premium template match.

Do not show lower match for better template.

Do not make system look inconsistent.

✅ Expected Result

After improvement:

✔ Suggested templates look smarter
✔ Match scores feel logical
✔ Premium templates appear premium
✔ Clear reason to switch
✔ Recommendation feels AI-powered
✔ User feels system is intelligent
✔ Upgrade flow feels natural
